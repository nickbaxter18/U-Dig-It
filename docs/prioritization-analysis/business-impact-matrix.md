# Business Impact Matrix

**Generated**: 2025-01-23
**Purpose**: Evaluate business value of each identified gap by assessing time savings, revenue impact, risk mitigation, and operational efficiency

---

## Impact Assessment Framework

Each feature is evaluated on:
1. **Time Savings** - Hours saved per week/month
2. **Revenue Impact** - Direct or indirect revenue impact
3. **Risk Mitigation** - Prevents errors, downtime, compliance issues
4. **Operational Efficiency** - Improves workflow, reduces manual work
5. **Customer Satisfaction** - Improves customer experience

**Scoring**: High (9-10), Medium (5-8), Low (1-4)
