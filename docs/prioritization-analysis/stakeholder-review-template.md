## Discussion Questions

### 1. Business Alignment

**Q1: Do these priorities align with business goals?**
- Follow-up: What would you change?
- Expected: General agreement, minor adjustments

**Q2: Are there business-critical features missing?**
- Purpose: Identify gaps
- Follow-up: What would you add?
- Expected: May identify additional features

**Q3: What's the minimum feature set needed for business operations?**
- Purpose: Define MVP
- Expected: Core admin functions

---

### 2. Resource Allocation

**Q4: Can we allocate resources for Phase 1 (Quick Wins)?**
- Purpose: Get approval for immediate work
- Expected: Yes, if ROI is clear

**Q5: What's the budget/timeline for full implementation?**
- Purpose: Understand constraints
- Expected: May have budget/timeline constraints

**Q6: Are there any dependencies or blockers?**
- Purpose: Identify risks
- Expected: May identify business blockers

---

### 3. Success Metrics

**Q7: How will we measure success?**
- Present: Success metrics document
- Ask: Do you agree with these metrics?
- Expected: General agreement, may add metrics

**Q8: What are the success criteria for each phase?**
- Purpose: Define acceptance criteria
- Expected: Clear criteria for each phase

## Approval Checklist

### For Stakeholder Approval

- [ ] **Business Goals Aligned** - Priorities match business objectives
- [ ] **ROI Confirmed** - Stakeholders agree with ROI estimates
- [ ] **Resources Approved** - Budget/timeline approved for Phase 1
- [ ] **Success Metrics Agreed** - Metrics are acceptable
- [ ] **Implementation Plan Approved** - Roadmap is acceptable
- [ ] **Dependencies Identified** - All blockers identified
- [ ] **MVP Defined** - Minimum feature set agreed upon
